Saint Martin's Island

Discover Saint Martin's Island

Saint Martin's Island, the only coral island in Bangladesh, is a tropical paradise located in the Bay of Bengal. Known for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and serene beaches, it’s a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike. Highlights include:

  • Coral reefs and marine biodiversity.
  • Relaxing on pristine, white sandy beaches.
  • Fresh seafood, especially locally caught lobsters and crabs.
  • Exploring the island’s traditional lifestyle and culture.
