Cox's Bazar

Explore the World's Longest Sea Beach

Cox's Bazar, located in southeastern Bangladesh, is famous for its 120 km long sandy beach, making it the longest sea beach in the world. It is one of the most visited tourist destinations in the country, offering a variety of attractions and activities. Highlights of Cox's Bazar include:

  • Laboni Beach - A hub for tourists to enjoy the sea view
  • Inani Beach - Known for its calm waters and coral stones
  • Himchari Waterfall - A serene spot surrounded by hills
  • Marine Drive - A picturesque road along the coastline
