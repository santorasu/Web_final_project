The Sundarbans

Experience the Magnificence of the Sundarbans

The Sundarbans, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is the largest mangrove forest in the world, located in southern Bangladesh. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, rich biodiversity, and as the home of the Royal Bengal Tiger. Highlights of the Sundarbans include:

  • The Royal Bengal Tiger and diverse wildlife
  • Mangrove trees and unique aquatic ecosystems
  • Boat tours through serene waterways
  • Stunning sunrise and sunset views over the forest
